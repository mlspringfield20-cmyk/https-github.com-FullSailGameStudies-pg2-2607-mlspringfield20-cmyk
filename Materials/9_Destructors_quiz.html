C++ Destructors Quiz

1. Which of the following statements about destructors is TRUE?

Correct! Destructors cannot have parameters and cannot be overloaded.
Incorrect.

2. What happens if you delete a Derived* through a Base* whose destructor is not virtual?

Correct! Deleting through a non‑virtual base destructor results in undefined behavior.
Incorrect.

3. Which destructor declaration correctly overrides a virtual destructor in a base class?

Correct! Both A and B are correct forms; C is invalid syntax.
Incorrect.

4. Which of the following is most accurate regarding exceptions in destructors?

Correct! Escaping an exception from a destructor during unwinding triggers std::terminate().
Incorrect.

5. In what order are members destroyed inside a class?

Correct! Members are destroyed in reverse order of declaration.
Incorrect.
